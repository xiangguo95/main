Xiangguo Liu

 

Hi, welcome to my page!

I'm a PhD in the ECE department at Northwestern University, supervised by Prof. Qi Zhu. My research interests include optimization, control and learning theories as well as applications on connected and automated vehicle and cyber-physical systems.

Prior to Northwestern, I received my B.E. from department of control science and engineering from Zhejiang University. I also visited the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor and was pleased to work with Prof. Neda Masoud.



Feel free to contact me for any comments on my research.
